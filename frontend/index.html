Travel Packing Checklist

Essentials

  • Passport
  • Wallet (Cash and Cards)
  • Phone and Charger
  • Travel Insurance

Clothing

  • T-Shirts
  • Pants/Shorts
  • Underwear

  • Deodorant
  • Sunscreen

Other

  • Snacks
  • Camera
  • Travel Guides/Maps
